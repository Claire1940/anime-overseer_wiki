In Anime Overseer, simply summoning a unit is not enough to survive the harder waves. You must actively upgrade and eventually evolve your fighters to unlock their true potential. <Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Establish a Core Lineup">
    Before investing heavily in evolution, build a team of 4-6 units that cover different roles: AoE damage, single-target DPS, and support/buffing.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Max Basic Upgrades">
    Use Gold earned from battles to upgrade your active units. A fully upgraded base unit is often more effective than a low-level evolved unit.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Gather Evolution Materials">
    Collect Soul Crystals, Super Soul Crystals, and specific food items like Meat or Onigiri. These are often rewarded from codes or high-level wave clears.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute Evolution">

  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Field Testing">
    Test your evolved unit in Endless Mode to see how their scaling affects your wave clear speed and adjust your placement strategy accordingly.
  </Step>
</Steps>
