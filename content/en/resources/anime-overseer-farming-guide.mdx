<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Trait and Soul Resource Management">

Beyond basic currencies, the **anime overseer farming guide** must cover specialized materials like Trait Discs and Soul Crystals. These items are the "endgame" of farming because they determine the ceiling of your unit's power. A unit with a "Godly" trait can outperform an evolved unit with a "Common" trait.

Trait Discs are used to reroll the innate abilities of your units. Since these are rarer than Gems, you should stockpile them until you obtain a Mythic or Secret rarity unit. Soul Crystals and Super Soul Crystals are specifically for the evolution system, which transforms your units into their ultimate forms.

| Material | Rarity | Acquisition Method | Optimal Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Trait Discs** | Rare | Codes, High-wave rewards | Save for S-Tier carries |
| **Soul Crystals** | Rare | Milestone rewards, Codes | Use for core team evolution |
| **Super Soul Crystals** | Very Rare | Major updates, Boss waves | Reserve for "Meta" evolutions |
| **Disc Rerolls** | Rare | Specific event codes | Fine-tune top-tier traits |

<Callout type="tip" title="Trait Strategy">
Do not chase "Perfect" traits early on. A "Good" or "Great" trait is sufficient to clear most Early Access content. Save your bulk Trait Discs for when the full game launches and the meta stabilizes.
</Callout>
