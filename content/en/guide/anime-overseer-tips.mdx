Evolution unlocks the true potential of your fighters, granting them massive stat multipliers, wider attack ranges, and devastating special abilities. However, because evolution materials are scarce, you must choose your targets carefully.

- **Confirm Unit Viability**: Only evolve units that are already a permanent fixture of your main team.
- **Check Material Requirements**: Accumulate the necessary Soul Crystals, Super Soul Crystals, and Relic Shards before attempting an evolution.
- **Optimize Traits Post-Evolution**: Wait until a unit is fully evolved before spending your premium Trait Rerolls, as evolution can alter a unit's optimal stat build.
- **Utilize Food Items**: Feed Meat, Onigiri, and Potatoes to your newly evolved units to quickly bring them back up to max level.
