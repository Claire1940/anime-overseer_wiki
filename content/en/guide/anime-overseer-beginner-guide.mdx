When building your first team, focus on a "Main Carry" strategy. Invest the majority of your in-game Gold into one or two powerful units that can handle the bulk of the enemies, while using cheaper units to catch any "leaks" that slip through your primary defense.

| Unit Role | Primary Function | Priority Level |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Main DPS** | Massive AoE damage to clear waves | **Highest** |
| **Boss Killer** | High single-target DPS for Wave 10+ | **High** |
| **Support** | Slowing enemies or boosting ally damage | **Medium** |
| **Filler** | Cheap units to block early-wave leaks | **Low** |

<Callout type="success" title="Strategic Placement">
