<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat Mechanics & Game Modes">

Surviving the enemy waves in Anime Overseer requires a solid understanding of battle mechanics. Every wave presents different enemy types, ranging from fast-moving scouts to massive bosses with high health pools. During a match, you must manage your active funds to upgrade deployed units, unlocking their wider range of attacks and elemental advantages.

### Strategic Game Modes

The game features multiple ways to play, catering to both solo tacticians and cooperative groups.

| Game Mode | Core Objective | Best For | Recommended Team Setup |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Story Stages** | Clear fixed waves of enemies to unlock new maps | First-time progression, basic Gold farming | 1 Main DPS, 1 AoE Clear, 1 Support |
| **Endless Mode** | Survive infinite waves of enemies with scaling difficulty | High-tier materials, leaderboard pushing | 2 AoE Carries, 2 Single-Target DPS, 2 Buffers |
| **Co-op Lobby** | Team up with friends to defend a shared base | Beating hard stages, testing hybrid compositions | Balanced mix of crowd control and boss-killers |

<Callout type="warning" title="Upgrade Priority">
Avoid spreading your Gold too thin during a match. It is almost always better to fully upgrade one or two high-impact carry units than to have several unupgraded units on the field.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Unit Evolution & Traits Management">

Unit evolution is the ultimate progression milestone in Anime Overseer. Evolving a unit completely transforms its moveset, vastly increases its base stats, and unlocks devastating special abilities. 

### The Evolution Pathway

To evolve a unit, you must meet specific requirements:
- **Max Base Level**: The unit must be fully leveled up using Gold and food items (like Meat and Onigiri).
- **Evolution Materials**: You need a designated amount of Soul Crystals, Super Soul Crystals, and specific secondary items.
- **Evolution Priority**: Always prioritize evolving your primary damage carry first, as they will provide the highest return on investment.

### Trait Rerolling Strategy

Traits provide passive multipliers that can make or break a unit's viability in high-level content. Use your Trait Discs and Rerolls wisely.
