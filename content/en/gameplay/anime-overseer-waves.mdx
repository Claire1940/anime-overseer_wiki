<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Beginner Guide to Wave Mastery">

Navigating the initial **Anime Overseer waves** can be challenging if you don't have a clear roadmap. The transition from a starter squad to a competitive team requires a disciplined approach to summoning and resource allocation. Follow these steps to establish a strong foundation for your account.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Team Assembly">
    Focus on gathering a full team of six units before worrying about high rarity. A balanced team of Rare or Epic units often outperforms a single Legendary unit that lacks support. Use your initial Gems from codes to fill all slots.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Wave Defense Execution">
    During battles, prioritize upgrading your primary AoE (Area of Effect) unit first. This ensures that groups of enemies are thinned out before they reach your base. Watch for "leaks" and adjust your placement to cover blind spots.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Resource Stockpiling">
    Do not spend your Trait Discs or Soul Crystals on starter units. Save these rare materials for units you intend to evolve. Focus on clearing as many waves as possible to farm Gold for basic level-ups.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Unit Evolution Planning">
    Identify your "Carry" unit—the one doing the most damage in your runs. Check the evolution requirements in the game menu and start targeting the specific food items or shards needed to unlock their stronger form.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Resource Management">
Avoid spreading your Gold too thin across all units. In the early game, it is more effective to have two highly upgraded units than six underpowered ones.
</Callout>
